Ægir Cooperative Service Offerings

The Co-op's services currently fall under four categories:

  • Aegir "vendor" Support provides the confidence that our experts will be available to help.
  • Training further builds your team's internal capacity.
  • A number of regular, discrete tasks are offered as Managed Services.
  • When your needs extend beyond any of the above, Consulting is available from the same team that maintains Aegir core and a significant number of contributed modules.

Excluded Services

We have specifically decided not to provide Aegir hosting services. Our reasoning here is that we want to help to build a thriving eco-system for Aegir. By providing hosting services, we would be competing against other participants in the eco-system, rather than supporting them.
